Hybrid Vertical Axis Wind Turbine (VAWT) - School Project

Page 1: Project Introduction

This project aims to design and develop a hybrid Vertical Axis Wind Turbine (VAWT) system suitable for generating small-scale renewable energy in low-electricity areas including our location which is "ras al hekma".

Project Goals

  • Design a compact, efficient VAWT for real-world use
  • Generate up to 40 watts of electrical power
  • Support locations with low electricity access
  • produce energy in an eco-freindly way

Why Hybrid VAWT?

  • Works well in variable wind conditions
  • Can generate energy regardless of wind direction
  • increase the efficency of the prototype that reaches 40%

Project Team

This project is being developed by students at stem high school for boys-6th of october, aiming to raise awareness about renewable energy solutions and build a working prototype for educational demonstration.
